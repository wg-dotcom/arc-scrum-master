Our Selection Rationale

Built for a Team That Grew from One to Nine — and Isn't Slowing Down

ChargeItSpot's project management team has scaled remarkably — from one development team to nine, each roughly seven developers strong. That kind of growth creates a very specific need: a mid-level Scrum Master who can own three of those teams solidly, run ceremonies with discipline, and navigate the natural work-process evolution that comes with fast scaling — without getting overwhelmed by it.

We heard clearly that this person needs to come from a product and software background. ARC's product lives at the intersection of software, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering — and the closer a Scrum Master is to that world, the faster they'll earn the trust of their teams. People who understand QA automation, APIs, integrations, and data analytics will have a natural advantage facilitating standups and refinements with your engineers.

We also understand the structure: this mid-level hire will be supported by a senior Scrum Master, so we focused on candidates who are growth-oriented, coachable, and strong enough to operate independently across daily ceremonies — while being collaborative enough to align with your evolving PMO standards. And because your standups and refinements happen in the morning to early afternoon (US Eastern), time zone overlap was a hard filter.

A note on our selection criteria: Scrum expertise is non-negotiable here — these are people who lead through the Agile framework, not just manage around it. We prioritized Jira fluency at the dashboard and reporting level, hands-on experience with QA and platform teams, and the kind of cultural fit and personality that integrates well with a tight-knit development team. Because ultimately, among technically qualified candidates, that's what will make the final difference.
